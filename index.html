Listing Management

Listing Manager

Anywhere Real Estate (now Compass International Holdings) • 2023–2025

A centralized listing platform syndicating listing data to downstream systems and local MLS systems.

The Challenge

Real estate agents spent significant time on manual listing entry across multiple systems and MLS platforms. The goal of Listing Manager was to own the listing data but also reduce duplicate workflows and reduce duplicate data entry and improve data quality.

What I Did

  • Owned full product lifecycle: discovery, PRDs, sprint-ready backlogs, staged pilots, and post-launch iteration
  • Led cross-functional team of ~11 across engineering, QA, UX, and data science
  • Analyzed pilot results (MN showed near-zero agent adoption) and led the pivot to a TC+-supported model
  • Designed the revised adoption strategy that shifted value delivery through transaction coordinators
  • Built OKR frameworks, quarterly roadmaps, and release notes to maintain exec alignment through the pivot

The Outcome

In the MVP rollout phase, the Transaction Coordinator model was validated in a Florida pilot and informed a scaling strategy for additional markets. The pivot demonstrated product judgment — recognizing when initial assumptions fail and redirecting resources toward a viable adoption path rather than forcing the original thesis. This was a solution until we fully integrated the listing platform to downstream systems.
